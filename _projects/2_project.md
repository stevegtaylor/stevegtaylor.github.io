Built and incubated a soil-based microbial fuel cell and installed it on the UCSC Farm for further experimentation. The performance of the microbial fuel cell is being monitered by a
 BeagleBone based architecture. The recorded data is being
transmitted to a remote server and is being published live on Jlab's website <a href="https://dirtviz.jlab.ucsc.edu/">Dirtviz</a>.
